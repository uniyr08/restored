What We're Doing Next
Priority 01

Add High-Value Local Keywords

Build on what's already working — add more geo-specific and material-specific terms. "Reclaimed wood Long Island", "solid oak furniture Massapequa", "custom dining table near me" — these are the searches we want to own.

Priority 02

Add Negative Keywords

Exclude competitor brand searches (Raymour & Flanigan, Crate & Barrel, Bob's Furniture) and price-driven queries ("cheap furniture") so every click goes to a buyer who values quality craftsmanship.

Priority 03

Scale Custom & Reclaimed Terms

"Custom kitchen table", "barn wood furniture" and "reclaimed hemlock" are showing early engagement. We'll push budget toward these to attract the highest-value custom order customers.

Priority 04

Recover Missed Call Leads

We'll share the call timestamps with the team so the showroom can ensure coverage during peak ad hours. Every missed call is a warm lead — we want to capture all of them going forward.

Priority 05

Continue PMAX Monitoring

PMAX is averaging $1.52 CPC and delivering daily calls and directions. We'll continue feeding it strong signals from both campaigns and monitor for further efficiency improvement.

Priority 06

Evaluate Budget Increase for Search

The Local Search campaign is showing strong signals in its first week. Once we have 2–3 weeks of conversion data, we'll present a recommendation to scale the budget and capture more of the local demand that's currently available.
